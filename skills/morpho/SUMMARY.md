# morpho
Supply, borrow and earn yield on Morpho — a permissionless lending protocol with $5B+ TVL.

## Highlights
- Supply assets to MetaMorpho vaults and earn yield with curated risk management
- Borrow from Morpho Blue isolated markets with competitive rates
- View positions with health factors and liquidation risk monitoring
- Browse markets with real-time supply/borrow APYs and utilization rates
- Repay debt with dust-free full repayment using borrow shares
- Supply collateral to Morpho Blue markets for borrowing capacity
- Claim rewards via Merkl distributor integration
- Support for Ethereum Mainnet and Base networks
