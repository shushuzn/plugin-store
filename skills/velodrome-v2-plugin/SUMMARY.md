# velodrome-v2-plugin
A DeFi plugin for swapping tokens and managing classic AMM liquidity positions on Velodrome V2, the largest DEX on Optimism.

## Highlights
- Swap tokens via Velodrome V2 Router on Optimism with slippage protection
- Support for both volatile (constant product) and stable (low slippage curve) pools
- Add and remove liquidity to earn trading fees from classic AMM pools
- Query real-time pool reserves and swap quotes without gas costs
- View LP token positions and estimated underlying token amounts
- Claim VELO gauge emissions from staked liquidity positions
- Auto-approval management with exact amounts (no unlimited approvals)
- Built-in support for major Optimism tokens (WETH, USDC, USDT, DAI, VELO, OP)
