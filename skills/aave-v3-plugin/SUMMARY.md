# aave-v3-plugin
A comprehensive DeFi plugin for lending and borrowing crypto assets on Aave V3, the leading decentralized liquidity protocol with over $43B TVL.

## Highlights
- Supply assets to earn competitive yield across Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, and Base
- Borrow against collateral with variable interest rates and flexible repayment
- Real-time health factor monitoring to prevent liquidation risks
- Enable efficiency mode (E-Mode) for higher LTV on correlated assets
- Manage collateral settings to optimize borrowing capacity
- Claim accrued rewards from Aave incentive programs
- View live market rates and APYs across all supported assets
- Comprehensive position tracking with USD-denominated portfolio view
