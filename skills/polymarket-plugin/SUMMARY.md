# polymarket-plugin
Trade prediction markets on Polymarket — buy and sell YES/NO outcome tokens on Polygon.

## Highlights
- Trade on prediction markets for real-world events on Polygon
- Support for both binary (YES/NO) and categorical markets
- Two trading modes: EOA (direct wallet) or POLY_PROXY (gasless via proxy)
- Browse markets by category: breaking news, sports, elections, crypto
- Check positions, manage orders, and redeem winning tokens
- 5-minute crypto up/down markets for short-term trading
- Automatic API credential derivation from onchainos wallet
- Built-in region restriction checking for compliance
