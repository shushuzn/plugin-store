# polymarket-plugin
Trade prediction markets on Polymarket — buy and sell YES/NO outcome tokens on real-world events using your onchainos wallet on Polygon.

## Highlights
- Trade binary (YES/NO) and categorical prediction markets on real-world events
- Support for both EOA and gasless proxy wallet trading modes
- Access to 5-minute crypto Up/Down markets for short-term trading
- Real-time market data, order books, and position tracking
- Automatic API credential derivation from your onchainos wallet
- Built-in region restriction checking for compliance
- Seamless USDC.e deposit and withdrawal on Polygon
- Winning token redemption after market resolution
