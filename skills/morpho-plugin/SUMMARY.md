# morpho-plugin
A permissionless lending protocol plugin for supplying, borrowing, and earning yield on Morpho with $5B+ TVL supporting both Morpho Blue isolated markets and MetaMorpho vaults.

## Highlights
- Supply assets to MetaMorpho vaults and earn yield with curated risk management
- Borrow from Morpho Blue isolated markets with flexible collateral options
- Withdraw from MetaMorpho vaults with partial or full redemption capabilities
- Repay Morpho Blue debt with dust-free full repayment options
- Supply and withdraw collateral to Morpho Blue markets
- View comprehensive positions with health factors across all markets and vaults
- Browse markets with real-time supply/borrow APYs and utilization rates
- Claim rewards via Merkl distributor integration
