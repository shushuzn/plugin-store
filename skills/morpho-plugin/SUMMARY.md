# morpho-plugin
A DeFi lending protocol plugin for supplying, borrowing, and earning yield on Morpho with $5B+ TVL across Ethereum and Base networks.

## Highlights
- **Supply & Earn**: Deposit assets to MetaMorpho vaults with curated risk management from Gauntlet, Steakhouse, and other experts
- **Borrow & Repay**: Access isolated lending markets on Morpho Blue with flexible collateral options
- **Portfolio Management**: View positions with health factors across both Blue markets and MetaMorpho vaults
- **Multi-Chain Support**: Operates on Ethereum Mainnet and Base with seamless chain switching
- **Market Discovery**: Browse markets and vaults with real-time APYs, utilization rates, and curator information
- **Reward Claims**: Claim incentive rewards via Merkl distributor integration
- **Safety Features**: Always dry-runs transactions first with detailed previews before user confirmation
- **Dust-Free Operations**: Smart repayment logic using borrow shares to avoid leaving residual debt
