# pancakeswap
Swap tokens and manage concentrated liquidity on PancakeSwap V3 across BNB Chain, Base, and Arbitrum.

## Highlights
- Multi-chain DEX trading on BNB Chain (BSC), Base, and Arbitrum
- Get real-time swap quotes across all fee tiers (0.01%, 0.05%, 0.25%, 1%)
- Execute token swaps via SmartRouter with slippage protection
- Add concentrated liquidity positions with customizable price ranges
- Remove liquidity and collect fees from existing positions
- View all active LP positions for any wallet address
- List and analyze pools for token pairs across fee tiers
- Built-in balance checks and safety validations before transactions
