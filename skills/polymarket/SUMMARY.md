# polymarket
Trade prediction markets on Polymarket — buy and sell YES/NO outcome tokens for real-world events on Polygon.

## Highlights
- Buy and sell outcome tokens (YES/NO shares) for prediction markets on Polygon
- Browse active markets with keyword filtering and live price data
- Check open positions with real-time P&L tracking across all markets
- Place limit orders or execute market orders with automatic slippage protection
- Cancel individual orders or all orders for specific markets
- Auto-derive API credentials from onchainos wallet with no manual setup
- Support for both binary markets (YES/NO) and categorical markets (multiple outcomes)
- Gasless order matching with on-chain settlement via Polymarket's CLOB exchange
