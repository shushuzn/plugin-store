# hyperliquid-plugin
Trade perpetuals on Hyperliquid with market/limit orders, TP/SL brackets, position management, and USDC deposit/withdrawal functionality.

## Highlights
- Trade perpetuals on Hyperliquid L1 with CEX-like speed and on-chain settlement
- Place market and limit orders with optional stop-loss and take-profit brackets
- Check positions, unrealized PnL, margin usage, and account balances
- Close positions with one command or set TP/SL on existing positions
- Deposit USDC from Arbitrum and withdraw back to Arbitrum
- Transfer funds between perpetual and spot accounts
- Support for both cross and isolated margin with customizable leverage
- Real-time price data for all Hyperliquid perpetual markets
